# 💎 Crafting

> [!tip] Overview
> Crafting is an artisan skill that allows players to create jewelry, pottery, and other items from raw materials. This skill is essential for creating valuable items and training other skills.

## 📋 What is Crafting?

Crafting involves:
- 💍 Creating jewelry
- 🏺 Making pottery
- 🥃 Crafting glass items
- 🧵 Creating leather items
- 🏑 Making battlestaves

## ⚒️ Training Methods

<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-3 gap-4">
<div>

### 💍 Jewelry Crafting
> [!note] Metal Working
> - Start with silver and gold bars
> - Create rings, necklaces, and amulets
> - Enchant jewelry for additional benefits
> - Best items: Gold rings, Sapphire rings

</div>
<div>

### 🏺 Pottery
> [!note] Clay Working
> - Create bowls, vases, and urns
> - Use soft clay and a pottery wheel
> - Fire items in a pottery oven
> - Best items: Unfired urns, Plant pots

</div>
<div>

### 🥃 Glass Making
> [!note] Glass Working
> - Create glass items using a furnace
> - Make glass orbs for battlestaves
> - Create decorative items
> - Best items: Glass orbs, Glass vials

</div>
</div>

## 📊 Experience Rates

<div class="grid grid-cols-1 md:grid-cols-2 gap-4">
<div>

### Basic Methods
> [!note] Early Levels
> - Jewelry crafting: 15-30 XP per item
> - Pottery: 20-40 XP per item
> - Glass making: 25-50 XP per item

</div>
<div>

### Advanced Methods
> [!note] High Levels
> - High-level jewelry: 50-100 XP per item
> - Urns: 40-80 XP per item
> - Battlestaves: 100-150 XP per item

</div>
</div>
